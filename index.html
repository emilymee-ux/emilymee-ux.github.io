Discover Perth's best Mexican food.

EL PUBLICO

El Publico

5pm-12am, 7 days a week

511 Beaufort St, Highgate WA 6003

El Publico offers some of the best Mexican food in Perth. In order to keep their food as authentic to the source as possible, the head chef went and visited Mexico for a month to learn all about the cuisine. El Publico also features fixed price menus, which are a great deal for groups. The bar has a wide selection of tequila and mezcal.

LA CHOLITA

La Cholita

Mon-Thurs 5pm-12am, Fri-Sun 12pm-2am

279 William St, Northbridge WA 6003

La Cholita is the perfect place to go when you want both great drinks and great food. Of particular note is their excellent lunch specials available all weekend. A trio of homemade salsas is served with each dish. The tortillas are served warm. Drinks are pricey but a much better deal during happy hour.

TWO GRINGOS

Two Gringos Mexican Grill

Mon-Sat 11:30am-9:30pm, Closed Sunday

257A Albany Hwy, Victoria Park WA 6100

Quick, easy, delicious. Two Gringos Mexican Grill is the most casual of the places on our list. That doesn't make the food any less delicious! They offer burritos, tacos, nachos, and more and you can customize every menu item since everything is made fresh right in front of you. A great place for lunch or dinner in Victoria Park.

LA VIDA URBANA

La Vida Urbana

Wed-Sun 4pm-11pm, Closed Monday and Tuesday

1/228 Carr Pl, Leederville WA 6007

The sister restaurant to El Publico, La Vida Urbana is undoubtedly one of the best Mexican restaurants in Perth. They take great care to pay homage to authentic Mexican cuisine, by sourcing the best possible ingredients. They also make many things from scratch, from cheese to salsa.

MEXICAN KITCHEN

Mexican Kitchen Fremantle

Fri-Sun 12pm-9:30pm, Mon-Thurs 5:30pm-8:30pm

19 South Terrace, Fremantle WA 6160

Mexican Kitchen in Fremantle is one of the first restaurants to pop up in WA, and is a Fremantle institution. The interior of the restaurant is colorfully decorated with a variety of Mexican textiles. Their guacamole is quite good, and the enchiladas are excellent. Check out their daily deals which can be quite a bargain!

CABALLITOS

Caballitos

Tues-Sun 4pm-1am, Closed Monday

26 Queen St, Perth WA 6000

Caballitos is really more of a bar than a restaurant, but that doesn't mean their tacos and totopos are any less delicious! They feature an impressive array of drinks, and some great happy hour specials. Once you've danced up an appetite, they have $5 taco Tuesdays! Their totopos (corn chips) and guac are the perfect with any drink.

ON THE BORDER

On The Border Mexican Grill

Sat-Sun 12-10pm, Tues-Fri 4:30-10pm, Closed Monday

28 Anchorage Dr, Mindarie WA 6030

On The Border in Mindarie is a great new Mexican restaurant in Perth. Their homemade guacamole is second to none, and the service is friendly. Try a burrito bowl for lunch on Saturday or Sunday! They also have a bar serving up great Margaritas. Don't miss the mural of Donald Trump on the wall!

ZOCALO'S

Zocalo's Restuarant

Tues-Sat 5-8:30pm, Closed Sunday and Monday

Safety Bay and, Penguin Rd, Safety Bay WA 6168

Zocalo's Mexican in Rockingham is a great place for dinner. It has a family friendly and festive atmosphere. The restaurant itself is decorated with various items collected on the owner's trips to Mexico. The queso is delicious and worth trying, as is their sangria.
